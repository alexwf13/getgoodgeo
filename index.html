Who this is NOT for

This won't work for everyone.
You definitely DON'T need this if:

You sell commodity products

If you're selling phone cases on Amazon, GEO won't pay off. AI works best for B2B, SaaS, professional services, healthcare, and complex offerings where trust matters.

Your online reputation is poor

AI is a mirror. If 80% of your reviews on G2, Yelp, or Google are negative, no AI will recommend you until you fix the fundamentals.

You need "leads by Friday"

GEO is structural work. First mentions in AI answers appear 2–3 months after implementation. This is a long game, not traffic arbitrage.
